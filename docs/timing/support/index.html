Support

Email details, screenshots, your device, your iOS version, and the screen where the issue happened to richducat@gmail.com.

Before You Email

  • Update to the latest version from the App Store.
  • Restart the app if a screen stops responding.
  • Include the screen you were on, what you expected, and what happened instead.
  • Reinstall only if you need to refresh a broken local session and understand local-only data may be removed.

Subscriptions and Billing

If you subscribed in the iOS app, use Settings > Manage Premium > Restore Mobile Purchase to restore access, and use the App Store subscription page to manage renewal or cancellation. Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple.

App Store billing is the only in-app purchase path presented in the iOS app.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly inside the app from the first launch screen by tapping Open Settings, then going to the red Danger Zone section near the top of Settings and tapping Delete Account.

If you cannot access the app, email richducat@gmail.com from the address tied to your account and include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line.

Response Window

We typically respond to support emails within 2 business days.
