CadetCatch

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

CadetCatch helps military families maintain private cadet watchlists, review photo match previews, save intel, and decode academy terminology. This policy explains what the app accesses and how data is handled.

Data stored on device

Cadet names, units, relationship labels, selected base photos, saved intel, scan history, and settings are stored locally on your device. The current iOS build does not require account creation.

Photos and camera

  • Photo library access is used only when you choose a base photo for a cadet.
  • Selected photos remain part of your local roster data on device.
  • Camera access may be used to capture a base roster photo if that flow is enabled.

Notifications

CadetCatch may ask for notification permission to alert you about priority match activity. Notification preferences can be managed in iOS Settings.

Purchases

CadetCatch Pro purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. Apple handles payment information; CadetCatch does not receive your full payment card details.

Sharing

CadetCatch does not sell personal data. Data may be shared only when you use system sharing controls, contact support, or when required to comply with applicable law.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent through eb28.co/#contact.
