Ring Tone Creator Pro Privacy Policy

Effective May 21, 2026.

Ring Tone Creator Pro lets users create ringtone-ready files on iPhone. User audio files stay on the device. The app does not upload imported songs, videos, recordings, or exported ringtone files to EB28 servers.

Account Data

The app uses Firebase Auth for email signup and Firestore for account records, including email address, free export limit, free exports used, app version, and a subscription-status mirror used for support.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. EB28 does not receive full payment card details.

Ads

Free users may see Google AdMob ads. Subscribers do not see ads while an active unlimited subscription is detected. Ad privacy choices can be managed inside the app.

Deletion

Users can delete their account in the app. This removes the Firebase account profile used by Ring Tone Creator Pro. Local audio files can also be deleted from the device by deleting app data.

Contact

Contact support at support@eb28.co.
