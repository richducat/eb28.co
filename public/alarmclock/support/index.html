Wake Up Ya Bish

Support

Need help with Wake Up Ya Bish? Use the notes below for common setup and troubleshooting steps.

Setup tips

  • Allow notifications when prompted so alarms can fire on device.
  • Enable calendar access only if you want to see your next upcoming event on the alarm screen.
  • Open the app after installing a new build and preview an alarm sound once to make sure audio is initialized on your device.

Troubleshooting

  • If alarms are muted, verify your selected sound and mute toggle.
  • If the calendar card is blank, confirm calendar permission is enabled in iOS Settings.
  • If a sound preview does not play, close and reopen the app and test the voice again.
