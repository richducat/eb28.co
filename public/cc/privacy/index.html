CadetCatch Privacy Policy

Effective June 6, 2026.

CadetCatch helps families manage local cadet rosters and check approved public photo pages for possible matches. Roster entries, selected profile photos, saved finds, notes, and source settings are stored on the user's iPhone.

Photo Sources

CadetCatch checks only secure public pages that are built in or added by the user. The app does not access private social accounts, private photo libraries, private websites, or logged-in pages.

Photos Permission

Photos permission is used only when the user chooses a profile photo for a private local cadet profile.

Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. EB28 does not receive full payment card details.

Tracking and Ads

CadetCatch does not use ads, tracking, push notifications, camera access, or location access in this build.

Data Deletion

Users can clear saved CadetCatch data inside the app. Removing the app from the device also removes local app data according to iOS behavior.
