Snapgrid Remix

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

Snapgrid Remix is a standalone puzzle game. It does not require an app account. The iOS app stores gameplay state on your device, can use Apple Game Center if you choose to sign in, and uses Google AdMob to show ads to non-premium players. The iOS app can also use Apple's AdServices attribution API to measure whether an install came from an Apple Ads campaign.

On-device data

Gameplay state, settings, best score, optional custom theme settings, notification preferences, Premium status, and Apple Ads campaign attribution fields returned by Apple may be stored locally on your device. Premium purchases are handled by Apple StoreKit.

Notifications

If you enable the optional daily reminder, the app schedules a local notification on your device. The reminder can be disabled in the app or in iOS settings.

Game Center

If you use Game Center, Apple handles your Game Center identity, leaderboard profile, and score submission under Apple's privacy policy. Snapgrid Remix does not create a separate account.

Advertising

The iOS app uses Google AdMob and Google's User Messaging Platform to show ads, manage consent choices where required, and offer an ad-free Premium upgrade. Google's Mobile Ads SDK may collect data such as IP address, device identifiers, advertising data, product interaction data, crash data, and performance data for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, and SDK/app functionality. The iOS app also uses Apple's AdServices attribution API to request privacy-preserving Apple Ads attribution. If Apple returns an attribution record, the app stores non-PII campaign fields such as campaign ID, ad group ID, claim type, and supply placement locally so Premium purchase and restore events can be diagnosed against Apple Ads performance. AdMob ads are not shown after Premium is active.

Data sales and tracking

We do not sell personal data. We do not create user profiles or operate our own advertising tracking system.

Network requests

The web version may load assets needed to serve the app. If a web user enters optional custom image URLs, the device may request those images directly from the URL host.

Children

Snapgrid Remix is a general audience game and is not directed to children under 13.
