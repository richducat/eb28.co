## Description

CadetCatch is a premium photo recovery command center for military parents and cadet families. Build a private roster, select a cadet, run fast scan modes, review match confidence, save important photo intel, and turn training moments into family-ready sitrep drafts.

The app also includes an offline academy jargon decoder that translates common military terms into plain English, making it easier for families to follow the cadet experience.
