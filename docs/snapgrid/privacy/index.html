Snapgrid Remix

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

Snapgrid Remix is a standalone puzzle game. It does not require an account and does not intentionally collect personal data.

On-device data

Gameplay state, settings, best score, optional custom theme settings, and notification preferences may be stored locally on your device.

Notifications

If you enable the optional daily reminder, the app schedules a local notification on your device. The reminder can be disabled in the app or in iOS settings.

Network requests

The App Store build is designed to run offline after install. The web version may load only assets needed to serve the app. If a web user enters optional custom image URLs, the device may request those images directly from the URL host.

Data sales and tracking

We do not sell personal data and do not use advertising or tracking SDKs in this app.

Children

Snapgrid Remix is a general audience game and is not directed to children under 13.
