Delete Your Account

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Delete Inside the App

From the first launch screen, tap Open Settings, then go to the red Danger Zone section near the top of Settings and tap Delete Account.

  1. Open Timing.Coach.
  2. Tap Open Settings.
  3. Scroll to Danger Zone.
  4. Tap Delete Account, or tap Sign In to Delete Account if you are signed out.
  5. Confirm the deletion prompt.

What Gets Removed

When account deletion completes, we remove the synced Timing.Coach account data we control for that account, including saved profile details, saved plans, reminders, history, compatibility profiles, uploaded profile images, and related account records tied to the deleted account.

What Does Not Automatically Change

App Store or Play Store purchase history is managed by Apple or Google and may remain visible in those accounts after deletion. If you have an active store subscription, cancel it through the relevant store account if you no longer want it to renew.

Need Help?

If you cannot access the app, email richducat@gmail.com from the address tied to your account and include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line.
