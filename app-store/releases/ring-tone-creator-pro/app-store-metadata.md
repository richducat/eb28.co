## Description
Ring Tone Creator Pro is a premium ringtone studio for iPhone.

Import audio from Files, extract sound from videos, use local unprotected music you own, record your own clip, or start from a polished tone template. Trim the best section, preview the waveform, add fade in and fade out, save your projects, favorite your best tones, and export ringtone-ready files.

What you can do:

- Create ringtone-ready files from audio, video audio, recordings, and starter tones
- Use a clean waveform editor with trim, fade, preview, rename, duplicate, favorite, and share tools
- Save and re-edit ringtone projects in your private library
- Claim three free exports after email signup
- Upgrade to Unlimited for unlimited exports and no ads
- Follow the built-in GarageBand install guide after export

User audio stays on your iPhone. Account and export-credit records are synced with Firebase so your free export limit is not just local device storage.

Unlimited is an optional auto-renewable monthly subscription. Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation and renews automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the current period ends. You can manage or cancel in Apple ID subscriptions.
