CadetCatch Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

CadetCatch is built for cadet families. This policy explains what information the app uses and how that information is handled.

Information you provide

  • Cadet names, unit labels, relationship labels, and selected profile photos.
  • Public source URLs you add or enable.
  • Saved possible matches and review notes kept in the app.

How information is used

CadetCatch uses selected profile photos on the device to compare faces against images found on approved public HTTPS source pages. Cadet profiles, saved matches, and notes are stored locally on the device.

Public source checks

The app checks only public HTTPS pages that are built in or added by the user. This build does not scan private social accounts, private photo libraries, private websites, or logged-in pages.

Payments

This build does not include ads, in-app purchases, or subscriptions.

Tracking and advertising

CadetCatch does not include third-party advertising and does not use data to track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Contact

Questions can be sent to support@eb28.co.
