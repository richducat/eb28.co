Timing.Coach

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Overview

This policy applies to the Timing.Coach mobile app and public support pages. You can browse public pages without signing in, but account, premium, and sync features require information you choose to save with us.

Information We Process

Depending on the features you use, we may process account details such as your email address, display name, and authentication provider.

We also process profile information and content you choose to save, including birth date, birth time, birth place, relationship entries, saved plans, reminders, goals, check-ins, history, notes, profile images, and preference data used to personalize planning views and compatibility workspaces.

If you use premium access, we may store entitlement and billing-status information needed to restore access. Payment credentials are handled by Apple, Google, or other payment providers and are not stored by us.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide personalized planning guidance, sync your profile, restore premium access, support your account, maintain service reliability, answer support inquiries, and keep saved plans, reminders, compatibility workspaces, and reflection history available in the app.

Service Providers

The app may rely on third-party providers for account authentication, cloud storage, purchase handling, hosting, and optional conversational AI features. These providers may include Apple, Firebase, Google-hosted service infrastructure we control, OpenAI for optional AI guidance after opt-in, and other infrastructure partners that help us operate the service.

If you enable optional AI guidance, the message you type and the planning context needed to answer it may be sent to OpenAI to generate a response. Planning, reminders, history, and compatibility features still work if you do not enable AI guidance.

Ads and Tracking

The current Timing.Coach mobile release is intended to ship without third-party ad SDKs and without cross-app tracking for advertising purposes. If that changes in a future release, we will update this policy and any required platform disclosures.

Retention and Deletion

We keep data for as long as needed to operate your account, deliver features you use, meet legal obligations, or resolve support and security issues. You can delete your account inside the app from Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact richducat@gmail.com.
